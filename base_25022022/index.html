Haadibadi believes that all people should have access to learning and find a stage for expression. We are a community-led initiative and we are committed to the work of building the movement for a publicly owned, free library system that is accessible to all and creating space for learning and expression through theatre.

Creative and Independent learning opportunities are becoming more expensive and inaccessible. Children from unprivileged and marginalised communities find it harder to access. The post pandemic world has further widened the gap of learning opportunities for these communities with the disadvantage of lack of available technology and against the run of everyday survival. There is a dire need to bridge these gaps in the communities and the country at large and connecting the dots for meaningful learning and voices of expression through art and performance.

Sanjay Purohit

Founder, Social Change Network

There is a need to bring various segments of society to the forefront to include them in the process of sustainable learning and development. Haadibadi is one such forum that provides the necessary space for children and youth to explore learning beyond the classrooms and at the same time enabling them to comprehend their role as an individual in societal transformation. Haadibadi's mission of introducing innovative and imaginative forms of learning to the children and youth of the working classes show their enthusiasm in participating in the development of education sector.

Murali Mohan Kati

Programme Convener, Samvada
