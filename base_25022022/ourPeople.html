Meet Our Team

Ravikiran Rajendran

Co - founder & Creative Director, Haadibadi

Ravikiran Rajendran is a Trustee, one of the Co - founders & Creative Director of Haadibadi. He is a theatre practitioner, actor, and teaches and is Guest Faculty at St. Joseph’s College, Bangalore.

Geetha M

Co - founder & Executive Director

Geetha M designs the learning content for the Library program. Geetha is an Educationist whose research interest is language pedagogy and children’s literature. Her interests include theatre, movies and asking children their favourite stories.

Bhushan

Co – founder and Trustee

Bhushan works as a manager at Inmobi.He also monitors Haadibadi's engagement for the corporate sector and its other projects. Bhushan is passionate about playing football and dancing.

Suriya

Library Facilitator

Suriya is passionate about working with communities, youth and children. She has an ear for many narratives from the members and enjoys the art and craft sessions with the younger members. Suriya believes that young girls must get more safe and free space for learning and growth.

Anand M

Library Facilitator

Anand works for a company through the week and has been volunteering at the community library during his weekend. Anand enjoys playing cricket, loves story telling and has instilled the love for the game of chess among our young members. He has also encouraged older members in the community to love reading.

Divya Hari Rao

Online Library Coordinator

Divya Hari Rao is working as a part-time lecturer in a private college in Bengaluru. She has been volunteering in creating, curating and editing the digital content for the website. Her interests include doodling, movies, and exploring new places to travel and eat.

Ashwini Ganeshan

Resource Facilitator

Ashwini works as Senior Project Engineer for a corporate company. She loves socialising, visiting beaches, AR Rahman's music and her recent love has been working out. She is also an avid reader, TV series buff and prides herself with her new found skills as a cook.
